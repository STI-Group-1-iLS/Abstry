Lhinden Shein Mandocdoc

I enjoy art but I am not very skilled at it. I enjoy drawing, painting landscapes, and creating DIY art, but the outcomes were not really impressive. I enjoy the thrill, satisfaction, and enjoyment that it provides. Contemporary art aids in the realization of these emotions. This course allows me to learn new things, expand my knowledge, and push myself to my limits. I'll admit that I'm not very good at this, but I enjoy trying new things. For me, art encompasses not only drawing, painting, participating in art challenges, and performing arts, but also the means by which I express myself and the pleasure I derive from doing so. This is the most exquisite work of art that a person can create.   

Lindenallee

 MARCH 2, 2022 
Linden tree is where my name comes from. I always compare myself to a linden tree because of its healing properties. Many common illnesses like cough, cold, and headache can be treated using different parts of this tree. I want to be as useful as this tree. I want to be someone’s healing and protection. I want to see those bunch of graceful trees with heart-shaped leaves, fragrant flowers and a small globular fruit hanging from a narrow leafy bract. I want to see those Avenue of Linden Trees at least once in a lifetime.
